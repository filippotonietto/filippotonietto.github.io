Echoes of Form

Development Period: September - November 2024
Technologies: Unity, C#, Blender
Team Size: Solo

Thesis project developed during my internship at Zucchetti SpA.

The idea behind the game came from a realization: STEM students have a very limited knowledge of graphic design concepts, which are actually very useful when it comes to the development of usable applications.

The project, therefore, aims to raise students' awareness of art, by presenting to the user a serious themed but engaging gameplay and examples taken from art history.

Babel

Development Period: 2 days in 2021
Technologies: Unity, C#
Team Size: 6

With a couple of other developers, I took part at the GMTK Game Jam 2021, which theme was "Joined Together", and developed "Babel", a puzzle game in which you have to attach various types of colorful humanoids to each other. Every character has its own weight and unique ways in which they distribute it, and your goal is to create the perfect-balanced tower and reach the top of the level!
